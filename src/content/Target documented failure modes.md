Every structural mechanism – rotation, caps, redistribution triggers, stewardship pairs, participation budgets – should target a specific, empirically observed failure pattern, not a theoretical risk. Design against what has actually gone wrong in comparable networks. See [[We design for specific failure modes]] for the failure patterns this architecture targets.
