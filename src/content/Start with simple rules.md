The entire design rests on a small set of conceptual tools – relational protocols with [[Every protocol has three inseparable layers|three layers]], [[Participating means enacting protocols|voluntary adoption with consequence]], [[Protocols are normative structures|shared commitments as a normative floor]]. From these, arbitrarily complex network structures can emerge bottom-up, without top-down determination. Resist the urge to pre-specify more than is needed.

## Sources

- Emergence from simple rules is a key feature of complex systems; see e.g. Holland (1998), *Emergence: From Chaos to Order*
