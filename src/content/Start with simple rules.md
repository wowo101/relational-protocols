The entire design rests on a small set of conceptual tools – relational protocols with [[Every protocol has three inseparable layers|three layers]], [[Participating means enacting protocols|voluntary adoption with consequence]], [[Norms are constructed through committed practice|shared commitments as a normative floor]]. From these, arbitrarily complex network structures can emerge bottom-up, without top-down determination. Resist the urge to pre-specify more than is needed.
