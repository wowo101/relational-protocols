Part of the [[Conflict engagement]] domain.

**Interface.** A clear pathway that governs how support is offered as conflict deepens: direct conversation → peer support → mediation → structural review. At each stage, the protocol defines who can be called upon and what their role is.

**Practice.** Regular training in empathic listening, de-escalation, and perspective-taking – offered across the network so that support capacity is distributed rather than concentrated in specialists.

**Orientation.** When patterns emerge from mediation that point to systemic rather than interpersonal causes, a structured process generates proposals for adjustment – ensuring that individual conflicts feed back into the network's structural learning.
