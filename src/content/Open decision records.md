Part of the [[Collective decision-making]] domain.

**Interface.** All mandates, role holders, and decision records are accessible to anyone in the network. This governs the relationship between those who decide and those affected by decisions, ensuring that authority is always visible.

**Practice.** Shared orientation metaphors, adapted from the ZRM's motto goal process: the network and its circles develop figurative, embodied expressions of shared direction – not analytical targets but images and phrases that speak to the whole person. These keep shared purpose alive as felt commitment rather than abstract mission statement.

**Orientation.** Regular narrative review, adapted from the Organising from Elsewhere (OfE) framework: examine the narratives embedded in our decision-making practices. Are our processes reproducing the paradigm of separation, or enacting interconnectedness?
