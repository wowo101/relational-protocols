The original seven living systems – resource distribution, decision-making, feedback and learning, conflict engagement, information flow, care and support, connection and community – do not disappear in a protocol-based architecture. They become *domains* within which clusters of related protocols operate.

Two domains have been developed as full worked examples with specific [[Protocols|protocols]]:

- [[Collective decision-making]]
- [[Conflict engagement]]

Four domains have been sketched at the principles level:

- [[Resource distribution]]
- [[Feedback and learning]]
- [[Information flow]]
- [[Care and support]]
- [[Connection and community]]
