The care and support field governs how the network attends to the wellbeing of its members. It includes protocols for:

**Mutual support** – how groups attend to each other's wellbeing.

**Welcoming** – how new members enter relationship with the network.

Each protocol requires [[Every protocol has three inseparable layers|ritual and compass layers]] attending to: who carries the relational weight, who feels at home, and whose care is invisible.

This field is sketched at the principles level; its specific protocols remain to be developed.
